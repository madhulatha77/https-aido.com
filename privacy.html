Privacy Policy

Learn how AIDO collects, uses, and protects your personal data.

1. Introduction

DPDPD Private Limited (“AIDO”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of users accessing the AIDO platform.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data.

By using the AIDO platform, you consent to the collection and processing of your personal data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

4. Use of Information

  • Assigning professionals

5. Data Sharing

Information may be shared with:

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access or misuse.

7. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary or required by law.

8. User Rights

  • Request deletion

9. Legal Compliance

We comply with applicable laws including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, India.
