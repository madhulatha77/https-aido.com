Medical Disclaimer

Important information about the role of AIDO and healthcare service responsibilities.

Last Updated: June 2026

Platform Role

AIDO is a digital healthcare coordination platform operated by DPDPD Private Limited.

The platform enables patients to book healthcare services delivered by partner hospitals and their authorized healthcare professionals.

Not a Healthcare Provider

AIDO is not a hospital, clinic, or medical practitioner.

The platform provides technology infrastructure for booking and coordinating healthcare services but does not directly provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or clinical care.

Healthcare Service Providers

Healthcare services booked through AIDO are delivered by licensed doctors, nurses, technicians, and other healthcare professionals associated with partner hospitals.

The respective partner hospital and its healthcare professionals remain responsible for medical supervision, treatment decisions, and patient care.

Emergency Situations

AIDO does not provide emergency medical services.

If you are experiencing a medical emergency, please immediately contact emergency services or visit the nearest hospital.

Medical Advice

Medical decisions should always be taken in consultation with qualified doctors or licensed healthcare professionals.

Information provided through the AIDO platform should not be considered a substitute for professional medical advice or hospital care where required.
