Medical Disclaimer

Important information about the role of AIDO and healthcare service responsibilities.

Last Updated: June 2026

Platform Role

AIDO is a digital healthcare coordination platform operated by DPDPD Private Limited.

The platform enables patients to book healthcare services delivered by partner hospitals and their authorized healthcare professionals.

Not a Healthcare Provider

AIDO is not a hospital, clinic, or medical practitioner.

The platform provides technology infrastructure but does not directly provide medical care.

Healthcare Service Providers

Services are delivered by licensed professionals associated with partner hospitals.

The hospital remains responsible for treatment and care.

Emergency Situations

AIDO does not provide emergency medical services.

Contact emergency services or visit nearest hospital.

Medical Advice

Always consult qualified professionals.

Platform information is not a substitute for medical advice.
