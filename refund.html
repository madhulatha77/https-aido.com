Support & Refund Policy

Understand how cancellations, refunds, and service policies work at AIDO.

Support & Refund Policy

1. Cancellation Before Healthcare Staff Dispatch

Full refund

2. Cancellation After Staff Dispatch

Depending on the service stage, partial or no refund may be applicable.

3. Cancellation by AIDO (Operational Reasons)

  • No partner hospital available within the service area after reasonable attempts → Full refund
  • Excessive service delay beyond scheduled time → Full refund

4. Completed Healthcare Services

Non-refundable

5. Refund Processing

Refunds are usually processed within 5–7 working days through the original payment method.
