Consent & Acknowledgement

Understand your responsibilities and consent when using AIDO healthcare services.

1. Voluntary Service Request

By booking through AIDO, you confirm that your request for healthcare services is made voluntarily and with informed consent.

2. Healthcare Service Providers

All services are provided by independent partner hospitals and their licensed healthcare professionals.

The hospital and its medical staff are solely responsible for diagnosis, treatment, and patient care.

3. Acknowledgement of Risks

Healthcare services may involve inherent medical risks depending on the patient’s condition and the nature of the procedure.

By proceeding, you acknowledge and accept these risks.

4. Accurate Medical Information

You agree to provide complete and accurate medical information, including allergies, medications, and medical history.

5. Platform Role (AIDO)

AIDO operates only as a technology platform.
